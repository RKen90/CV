About me

I am a highly motivated and professional graduate, with experience in teaching, heritage, and retail sector. I have excellent organisational skills, and am reliable and confident in all situations.

I have a strong education and can adapt quickly to new situations. I graduated with Honours in History from the University of Glamorgan and subsequently studied an MA in English at Cardiff Metropolitan University. I have developed a passion for Computer Sciences and their use in the classroom and am preparing to begin studying an MSc in Computer science with software development.

I have completed a PGCE teaching degree where I taught a minimum of 80% of lessons, covering the whole curriculum. In my final teaching observations I received excellent in a number of areas, particularly my assessment and differentiation, demeanour, and leadership within a classroom. I have been praised for the standard of my English lessons and the quality of resources that I produce. I have spent the last two years working in International schools in Egypt where I taught Year 6, 7 and 8 classes full time. I was head of English for Primary and helped to introduce new schemes into the school. I was also able to introduce a new behaviour management policy to assist the school. I am looking to further develop my teaching career in the international field.

I have become passionate about Computing and Programming and am developing my skills to persue a career in this industry.
